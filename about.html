About Us

Our history, our beliefs, and the vision that drives everything we do as Ambassadors for Christ.

Our Story

How it all
began

The Ambassadors Club at SDA Church Embakasi Central was founded in 2023 with a simple but powerful conviction: that young people are not just the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today.

What started as a small gathering of passionate youth has grown into a vibrant community of over 45 members committed to worship, service, and leadership. We believe that every young person has a God-given calling, and our club exists to help them discover and live it out.

From choir ministries to community outreach, from Bible study to fellowship nights — everything we do flows from one source: the love of Christ and the desire to reflect it in Embakasi and beyond.

Our Mission

To empower young people at SDA Embakasi Central to live out their faith boldly — through worship, service, and community — and to equip the next generation of church leaders.

Our Vision

A generation of young Adventists in Embakasi who are rooted in Scripture, alive in fellowship, and transforming their communities for Christ — one life at a time.

Our Motto

"One for Christ"

United in purpose, diverse in gifts — every Ambassador committed to a single cause: making Christ known in Embakasi and to the ends of the earth.

What We Believe

Grounded in
Scripture

As a Seventh-day Adventist youth ministry, our beliefs are anchored in the 28 Fundamental Beliefs of the SDA Church. Here are the convictions that shape everything we do.

01

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired Word of God — our supreme authority in all matters of faith, doctrine, and practice.

02

The Trinity

One God exists eternally as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — three persons, united in nature and purpose.

03

God the Father

The eternal Father is Creator and Sustainer of all things — just, holy, gracious, and abounding in love.

04

God the Son

Jesus Christ, truly God and truly human, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, and rose again for our salvation.

05

God the Holy Spirit

The Spirit convicts, renews, and empowers believers, and equips the church with gifts for ministry and witness.

06

Creation

God created the heavens and earth in six literal days and rested on the seventh, establishing the Sabbath.

07

Nature of Humanity

Made in God's image with body, mind, and spirit, humanity fell into sin but can be restored through Christ.

08

The Great Controversy

All humanity is caught up in the cosmic conflict between Christ and Satan over God's character and law.

09

Life, Death & Resurrection of Christ

Christ's perfect life, atoning death, and bodily resurrection provide the only basis for our salvation.

10

The Experience of Salvation

Through faith in Jesus we are justified, adopted as God's children, and born again by the Holy Spirit.

11

Growing in Christ

Walking daily with Jesus in prayer, His Word, and worship, we grow into His likeness and serve others.

12

The Church

The church is a community of believers — the body of Christ — called to witness, worship, and welcome all.

13

The Remnant and Its Mission

In the last days, a remnant people keeps God's commandments and proclaims the everlasting gospel to the world.

14

Unity in the Body of Christ

In Christ, distinctions of race, culture, and status fall away — we are one fellowship, equal and united.

15

Baptism

By baptism through immersion, believers confess faith in Christ's death and resurrection and join His church.

16

The Lord's Supper

Communion and foot-washing express faith in Christ, renewed cleansing, and humble service to one another.

17

Spiritual Gifts and Ministries

The Holy Spirit gives every believer gifts for ministry, building up the church and serving others in love.

18

The Gift of Prophecy

Scripture testifies to the gift of prophecy as a mark of God's remnant church, guiding it in truth.

19

The Law of God

The Ten Commandments express God's love and will, remaining the standard for Christian conduct today.

20

The Sabbath

The seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) is God's holy day of rest — a gift, a sign, and a delight we embrace with joy.

21

Stewardship

Time, talents, and treasures are entrusted to us by God — including our health, finances, and this planet.

22

Christian Behavior

We aim to think, feel, and act in ways that reflect Christ, honoring our bodies as the Spirit's temple.

23

Marriage and the Family

Marriage, established by God between a man and a woman, reflects Christ's love and commitment to His church.

24

Christ's Ministry in the Heavenly Sanctuary

Christ ministers on our behalf in the heavenly sanctuary, carrying out a work of judgment before His return.

25

The Second Coming

Jesus Christ is coming again — literally, visibly, gloriously. This hope defines our urgency to preach and serve.

26

Death and Resurrection

Death is an unconscious sleep until Christ returns, when the righteous will be raised to eternal life.

27

The Millennium and the End of Sin

Christ reigns with His saints for a thousand years, after which sin and sinners are forever removed.

28

The New Earth

God will make His home with His people on a renewed earth, free of suffering and death, forever.
