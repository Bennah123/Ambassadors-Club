Our Calling

We are Christ's Ambassadors

"We are therefore Christ's ambassadors, as though God were making his appeal through us."

— 2 Corinthians 5:20

The Ambassadors Club at SDA Church Embakasi Central exists to empower young people to live out their faith, serve their community, and develop leadership skills rooted in biblical principles. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we prepare the next generation of church leaders.
