Our Calling

Built on a
living word

The Ambassadors Club exists to empower young people at SDA Embakasi Central to live out their faith boldly — through worship, service, and community. We are not spectators. We are participants in something eternal.

Our Story →

Leadership

Developing youth leaders for church and community through mentorship and practice.

Service

Outreach programs across Embakasi and beyond — health, welfare, and the gospel.

Worship

Music ministry and spiritual growth that lifts hearts and honours God.

Fellowship

Building lasting friendships in Christ across generations and backgrounds.
